About Me

My name is Vanessa M. Shalkey, and I'm a full-stack web developer. I love gaming, shredding the slopes, and roughing it with my two puppies! Learn more about me in the section below. You can hover over certain words or click on the links to get more information.

Computer Coding

I've earned a double black belt for web development from Coding Dojo in Bellevue, WA.

I specialize in writing code for the MAMP, MEAN, and Ruby on Rails stacks.

You can see a breakdown of my skills and samples of my code by clicking on the links or continuing to scroll!

Teaching Experience

Before coding, I trained with Teach For America to teach at public high schools in Hawaii.

I taught English at McKinley High School in Honolulu. As the Speech and Debate Club advisor, I was psyched that every member of our team qualified for the State Tournament!

At Waianae High School I taught Social Studies and College Prep in the New Tech Academy and English in the Sophomore Academy the following year. Both years I taught in technology equipped classrooms using apps such as Schoology, Socrative, and ClassDojo.

College Education

I graduated from Penn State in University Park, PA with a BA in English and International Politics.

As a Schreyer Honors Scholar and a Paterno Fellow, I wrote my senior thesis on the confessional and modernist poets.

Throughout my four years at PSU, I raced on the Ski Team, rode on the Equestrian Team, and worked at the Penn State Golf Courses.
